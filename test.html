DNS (Domain Name System) converts human-readable domain names into IP addresses, ensuring seamless web access. It's a foundational technology for internet navigation and business availability.

DNS resolves domain names to IP addresses using a hierarchical and distributed naming system. Includes root, TLD, authoritative servers, and caching layers. Misconfigurations can lead to resolution failure or hijacking.
